what is tasks simplified?

What if there was a task or to do list that simply offered just that. One list, easy task entry, easy deletion, and absolutely no clutter. That is what Tasks Simplified is and it is beautiful.

simple

Add tasks by speech, text, or even via share from outside Tasks Simplified. You are only one click away from adding a new task.

beautiful

Designed to not only be simple, but also elegant. Taking advantage of MonoDroid ActionBar Tasks Simplified brings the beauty of the action bar to every device. On HoneyComb and above Tasks Simplied embraces the core Holo Light theme from Google.

adorable

Hands down this is the most adorable task and to do list that you will ever use!

available

Tasks Simplified officially supports Android 2.1 (Froyo)+ and is available on Google Play or the Amazon App Store for FREE!!!!:
